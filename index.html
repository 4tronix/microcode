Program your micro:bit V2 with MicroCode!

  1. Connect your micro:bit V2 to your computer. (micro:bit V1 is not supported)
  2. Download MicroCode to the MICROBIT drive.
  3. Wait for the micro:bit to restart
  4. , choose "BBC micro:bit CMSIS-DAP" and press 'Connect' in the dialog.
